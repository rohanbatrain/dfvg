# Mobile App

The DFVG mobile app allows you to monitor and control jobs from your iOS or Android device.

## Installation

### Prerequisites

- **Desktop App Running**: Ensure the DFVG desktop app is running on your computer.
- **Same Network**: Connect your mobile device to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer.

### iOS

1. Install **Expo Go** from the App Store.
2. In the DFVG desktop app, navigate to the **Mobile** tab.
3. Use your phone's camera to scan the displayed QR code.
4. Tap the notification to open the app in Expo Go.

### Android

1. Install **Expo Go** from the Google Play Store.
2. In the DFVG desktop app, navigate to the **Mobile** tab.
3. Open Expo Go on your phone and tap **Scan QR Code**.
4. Scan the QR code displayed in the desktop app.

## Features

### Dashboard

- **Status Overview**: View active, completed, and failed jobs at a glance.
- **Quick Actions**: Start common workflows with a single tap.

### Job Monitoring

- **Detailed Progress**: Track percentage complete, current file, and time remaining.
- **Notifications**: Receive alerts when jobs complete or encounter errors.

### Remote Control

- **Pause/Resume**: Control active jobs remotely.
- **Cancel**: Stop unwanted jobs.
- **Retry Failed Jobs**: Quickly restart failed tasks.

## Troubleshooting

- **Connection Issues**: Ensure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network. Check firewall settings on your computer.
- **QR Code Scanning**: Make sure your phone's camera lens is clean and the QR code is clearly visible.
